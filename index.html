About

The "Alpha Code Team," born from Mr. Sheyi's vision, embarked on a transformative journey fueled by passion and determination. From their early days as learners to becoming commercial programmers, their milestones are a testament to growth and creativity. The team expanded its horizons beyond the normal perspectives, impacting not only their school but also younger students. Invited to teach programming, they became mentors, passing on their knowledge and passion. The Alpha Code Team was conceived by Mr. Sheyi with a noble purpose to equip young boys with life-coping programming skills. Developing websites proposed by the competition board, they demonstrated innovation, problem-solving, and teamwork. These young boys have stepped into the realm of boundless possibilities with their mesmerizing web designs that transcend the ordinary, creating digital experiences that captivate and engage the mind of everyone.
